# Indexing Pipeline: Deep Codebase Understanding in 90 Seconds

## The Problem

AI coding assistants are powerful -- but they don't know your codebase. Every session starts from scratch. They miss your architecture, ignore your conventions, and produce generic code that doesn't belong in your project.

Developers end up spending more time correcting AI output than they save using it. The promise of AI-assisted development falls short because the AI lacks the one thing that matters most: context about your specific codebase.

## The Solution

Carto's Indexing Pipeline automatically analyzes your entire codebase and builds a layered semantic understanding -- the kind of knowledge a senior engineer develops over months. In under 90 seconds.

No manual documentation. No configuration. No maintenance. Carto scans your code, uses AI to understand architecture and patterns at every level, and stores that understanding for instant use by any AI coding assistant.

## Key Benefits

- **90-second setup.** Point Carto at your codebase and it handles everything. Full understanding, ready to use, in under two minutes.
- **Always current.** Incremental indexing means only changed files get re-analyzed. After the first run, updates take seconds.
- **30+ languages.** Go, TypeScript, Python, Java, Rust, and more. Production-grade parsing that understands code structure, not just text.
- **Deep understanding.** Carto doesn't just list files. It maps how components connect, identifies patterns your team follows, and captures the architectural decisions that shape your project.

## How It Works

1. **Scan** -- Discovers your project structure and identifies all relevant code.
2. **Analyze** -- AI models extract meaning at multiple layers: functions, modules, cross-component relationships, and system architecture.
3. **Store** -- Packages the understanding for instant retrieval by AI assistants.

## Who It's For

Engineering teams using AI coding assistants who want AI-generated code that actually fits their project. Engineering leaders who want consistent, high-quality AI-assisted development across their organization.

## Get Started

Run a single command. Carto handles the rest. Your AI assistants will immediately start producing code that follows your architecture, respects your conventions, and understands your domain.
