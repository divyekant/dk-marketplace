# Multi-Provider LLM Support: Your AI Provider, Your Choice

## The Problem

AI-powered developer tools typically lock you into a single provider. When that provider raises prices, changes terms, or doesn't meet your compliance requirements, you face a painful migration. You're also overpaying -- most tools use the same expensive model for every task, whether it's summarizing a simple function or mapping complex system architecture.

## The Solution

Carto works with Anthropic, OpenAI, and Ollama out of the box. Switch providers with a single configuration change. Mix providers to optimize for cost and performance. Or run entirely on local models for complete data privacy.

Carto's two-tier strategy automatically assigns the right model to the right task. High-volume, straightforward analysis goes to fast, affordable models. Deep architectural reasoning goes to the most capable models. You get the best results without overspending.

## Key Benefits

- **Zero lock-in.** Three providers supported today. Switch anytime, no migration required.
- **Lower costs.** The two-tier model strategy uses affordable models for bulk work and reserves premium models for tasks that need them. Teams typically see significant cost savings compared to single-model approaches.
- **Run fully local.** Ollama support means your code never has to leave your network. Ideal for regulated industries, government contractors, and security-conscious organizations.
- **Future-ready.** As the AI landscape evolves, Carto evolves with it. New providers and models can be added without disrupting your workflow.

## How It Works

Configure your preferred provider in one place. Carto handles the rest:

- **Fast tier** handles high-volume analysis (function summaries, code patterns) with efficient models.
- **Deep tier** handles complex analysis (architecture mapping, cross-component relationships) with capable models.

Mix and match providers across tiers, or use the same provider for both.

## Who It's For

Teams that value flexibility, cost control, and data sovereignty in their AI tooling. Organizations in regulated industries that need local-only processing. Engineering leaders who refuse to bet their infrastructure on a single AI vendor.

## Get Started

Set your provider, set your API key, run Carto. That's it. Switching later is just as simple.
